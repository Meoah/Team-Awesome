Controls:

WASD/Arrow keys to move and aim on the water.

Press and hold "Space" to begin fishing.

Release "Space" to throw lure.

Press "Space" again to reel in a bite.

Use WASD/Arrow keys in correlated combination to fish within the timer.

If the arrow is RED, press the OPPOSITE direction.


Synopsis: You play as Jeremy the Jerboa who must sell fish to pay off his Mob Boss landlord Failure to pay rent will have Jeremy the Jerboa evicted and have his knees broken
